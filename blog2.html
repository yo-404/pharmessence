The Science Behind Our Signature Moisturizer

At PharmEssence, every jar of our signature moisturizer is a fusion of science and nature. Developed by a licensed pharmacist and an esthetician, our formula blends high-performing ingredients with proven skincare benefits. We started with a single goal: create a cream that delivers real results for real skin—no hype, no harshness.

Our moisturizer includes kokum butter for deep hydration, argan oil for anti-aging properties, and jojoba oil to mimic skin's natural sebum. Each ingredient was chosen for its clinical benefits and ability to soothe, repair, and protect. Together, they offer a smooth, lightweight texture that absorbs instantly while locking in moisture all day long.

This formula was tested on diverse skin types, including oily, sensitive, and combination skin. We listened to feedback, adjusted our ratios, and landed on the perfect balance. The result? A moisturizer that feels luxurious, works hard, and fits beautifully into your daily routine—whether you're fighting dryness, breakouts, or just want to glow.
